Drag around in the frame to rotate the svg. The demo uses only quaternions to rotate the two triangles.

It’s difficult to do visible surface determination in SVG for various reasons. Above, the triangles are sorted by their z-value and then they’re drawn back-to-front. This is known as the painter’s algorithm and will only work in certain cases.
